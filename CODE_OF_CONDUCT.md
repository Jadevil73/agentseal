# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AgentSeal is built by people who care about security, and we hold our community to the same standard. We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

This applies to all AgentSeal community spaces: GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channels where community members interact.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Give and receive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Take responsibility for your words and actions
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Credit the work of others when building on it

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory or derogatory comments based on personal characteristics
- Publishing someone's private information without their explicit permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contribution processes
- Sexualized language or imagery in any community space
- Trolling, insulting comments, or deliberately inflammatory behavior
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing [hello@agentseal.org](mailto:hello@agentseal.org). All reports will be reviewed and investigated. We will maintain confidentiality to the extent possible.

## Enforcement

Community maintainers will take appropriate action in response to violations. This may include:

1. **Correction** - A private conversation to clarify expectations
2. **Warning** - A formal notice that the behavior must stop
3. **Temporary restriction** - Temporary loss of access to community spaces
4. **Permanent ban** - Permanent removal from all community spaces

The severity of the response will match the severity of the violation. Repeat violations will result in escalated responses.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all AgentSeal community spaces and to any situation where someone is representing the project publicly.
